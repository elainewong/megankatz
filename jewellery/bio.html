Biography

Megan Katz has completed a Bachelor of Fine Arts at the Nova Scotia College of Art and Design University in Halifax, with a major in Jewellery Design and Metalsmithing. Taking advantage of opportunities available to her, Megan has completed an exchange program at the Glasgow School of Art, and has attended workshops with international artists Noam Ben-Jacob and Donald Stuart.
